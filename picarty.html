picArty, an iOS & Android App

Background (2011)

-Estimated more photos were taken every 2 mins then all photos developed in the 1800s.
-Rise of the app economy.
-Fatbooth was in top 25 paid apps.
-"Photos Are The New Kill App: Why photography is every tech product's most valuable feature" - Fast Company's Farhad Manjoo.
-Passion to develop a software led to my 1st camera app.
-Developed branding & UI.
-Leveraged freelancer.com, GitHub.
-Project managed offshore teams.
-Distributed picArty on iTunes and Google Play.

picArty

-picArty creates portrait montages by mixing and matching users' faces with professionally photographed masks.
-Artistes simply select a mask, line up their face, strike a pose & click.
-12 entertaining masks.
-Great for dinner parties, blind dates & FB posts.

Business Learning

-picArty's original price was $1.99 with limited downloads.
-Pivoted to freemium model one mask plus in-app purchases.
-Gamed itunesconnect to increased downloads = 130K organic downloads in one year.
