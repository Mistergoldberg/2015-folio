picArty (iOS & Android App)

Background

  • Estimated more photos were taken every 2 mins than all photos developed in the 1800s.
  • Rise of the app economy.
  • Fatbooth was in top 25 paid apps.
  • Photos Are The New Kill App: Why photography is every tech product's most valuable feature - Fast Company's Farhad Manjoo.
  • Passion to develop a software led to my 1st camera app.

picArty

  • picArty creates portrait montages by mixing & matching users' faces with professionally photographed masks.
  • Artists select a mask, line up their face, strike a pose & click.
  • 12 entertaining masks.
  • Great for dinner parties, blind dates & Facebook posts.
  • Developed with freelance developers; code managed on GitHub.
  • Distributed on iTunes and Google Play.

Business Learning

  • User feedback and testing provided dozens of new feature ideas.
  • Price point dictates adoption: picArty lauched at $1.99, limited downloads resulted. Pivoted to a freemium model (in-app purchases), adoption increased by 1,000%.
  • Regularly cycling prices consistently triggered spikes in sales.
  • Android users are significantly less likely to purchase apps than iOS users.
  • Freelance developers are a price-effective tool to prototype apps.
