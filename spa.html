Lay-Z-Spa App Control

Customer Service & Sales Feedback

  • Water maintenance is time consuming.
  • Expectations of power usage are unclear.
  • Power-saving feature requires excessive planning.

Target Users

  • Middle-aged professionals, time-constrained.
  • Desire immediate use of spa.
  • Desire lower cost of ownership.

User Research

  • 90% no experience with water chemistry.
  • 70% let water quality fall below healthy levels.
  • 100% wanted to use spa but did not activate 12 hours before intended use.
  • 30% saw an unacceptable rise in power usage.
  • 75% used spa 4 x week.

Outcome

  • Low-fidelity mockups well received in testing.
  • Straight-forward and easy-to-use design.
  • Non-scheduled usage still requires preplanning.
