Push Buttom Sprinkler Pools

Sales and Marketing Feedback

-Small ring pools are high sales volume items.
-Features add profit potential, result in volume orders.
-Pricing is key for parents, therefore cost dictates success.

Target Users

-Kids 3+
-Inquisitive, sensory motivated.
-Refined movement skills.
-Control handles, levers and switches.

User Research

Toy of choice:
-100% simple sprinkler, 15% color toys, 75% interactive toys.
Actions:
-72% played with shut off valve, 5% sat on sprinkler, 13% picked up sprinkler, 10% were scared.

Outcome

-Foot activated valve removed fine motor issues.
-Full body weight activated sprinkler.
-Popular themes for target users allowed for a turtle, elephant and dragon.
-High volume orders achieved.
