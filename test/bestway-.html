Bestway Branding

Background

In 2003 I started the adventure of a lifetime when I was blessed with the opportunity to be the first foreign-born designer for Bestway Inflatables, a Chinese sporting goods and toy manufacturer and the world's second largest inflatables manufacturer. My responsibilities involved simply everything.In 2004 I transitioned to the role of creative director and over the course of a decade helped to build one of China's first self-made brands.

Photo Production

One job perk I especially enjoy is flying the world taking photos of people having fun. I coordinate all aspects of the shoots including hiring talent, sourcing production crews and locations, budget and risk management. On set I direct crew, art direct photos, motivate talent and kiddie wrangle little people in search of smiles and perfect pictures.

Design For Print

With photos in-hand it's back Shanghai where I art direct my team to design catalogues, packages, tradeshow booths and advertisements for Bestway's global operations.

Catalogues

Each season I produce catalogues to present Bestway's core product categories. These catalogues are key sales tools that all the sales representatives in our global network use to generate orders.

It has been fun and a great challenge to cultivate the Bestway brand for globe distribution.

During catalogue production I work as the lead creative director and coach a team of art directors to generate concepts and then I present those concepts to our sales and marketing teams. Once a direction is confirmed I art direct the photos and produce the final presentations.
