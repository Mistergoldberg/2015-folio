Mash Up (iOS App)

Background

  • picArty user experience research revealed areas to explore further app development.
  • The ability to create and customize masks in the app was a key feature that continually surfaced during testing.

Mash Up

  • Mash Up is a social photo app to remix photos and collaborate with others.
  • Users download a mask, line up their face, strike a pose, and share the new mask.
  • App includes a mask creation tool, library manager, and portfolio section.
  • Push notifications re-engage users everytime one of their masks is modified and shared.
  • Gallery and emails of popular masks creates competition to drive engagement.
  • Developed with freelance developers; code managed on GitHub; Amazon Web Services provided infrastructure.
  • Distributed on iTunes.

Business Learning

  • Agile methodology provides transparency to developer progress and enables rapid evolution of features.
  • Product use logs reveal insights not found during user testing.
  • Amazon Web Services S3 and EC2 provides low-cost infrastructure.
  • Lean Startup principles forces focus on commercial viability, rapid product evolution.
