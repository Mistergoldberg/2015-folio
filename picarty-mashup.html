Mash Up, an iOS App

Background

-picArty version one user-experience research revealed opportunity areas to explore further app development.
-The ability to create and customize masks in the app was a key feature that continually surfaced during testing.

Solution

-Mash Up, a social photo app to remix photos and collaborate with others.
-The app includes a mask creation tool, library manager, push notifications and portfolio section.

Business & Development Learning

-Managed client and server side development teams.
-Used Amazon Web Services.
-Leveraged agile development strategies.
-Incorporated lean startup principles.

Sidebar
-Due to iOS updates and cash flow issues, I was forced to abandon this pet project.
