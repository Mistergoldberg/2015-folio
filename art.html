The Pixilation Player

Background

  • Began shooting in 2001 when attacked and robbed in Shanghai.
  • Shooting for over a decade, amassed +10K photographs.
  • DC-530 shoots 640x480 photos.

Pixilation Player

  • The pixilation player plays photos in automatic, rapid succession; or controlled, frame by frame.
  • Photos are uploaded and organized, orchestrated to a soundtrack.

Definition: Pixilation (from pixilated) is a stop motion technique where live actors are used as a frame-by-frame subject in an animated film, by repeatedly posing while one frame is taken and changing pose slightly before the next frame.

Insights

  • New video technology produces individual frames of photographic quality.
  • Photography in the age of mass storage: cost to produce and store photos has become irrelevant.
  • New art form is emerging as photography and videography merge; interactivity becomes a key component.
