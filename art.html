The Pixilation Player

Background

-Began shooting in 2001 when robbed and attacked in Shanghai.
-Been shooting for over a decade.
-DC-530 shoots 640x480px photos.


Opportunity

-Amassed tens of thousands of images.
-Press fast-forward and still photos morph to animated pixilations.


Definition: Pixilation (from pixilated) is a stop motion technique where live actors are used as a frame-by-frame subject in an animated film, by repeatedly posing while one or more frame is taken and changing pose slightly before the next frame or frames.


Solution

The pixilation player plays photos in:
-Automatic, rapid succession.
-Controlled, frame by frame.
