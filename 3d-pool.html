3D Play Pool

Sales and Marketing Feedback

-Rectangle inflatable pools are a profit-losing commoditized items.
-Innovative features and integrated play patterns create profitability.

Target Users

-Kids 5+
-Refined motor skills, moving, swimming, talking.
-Competitive game play.
-Complex patterns are a must.

User Research

Quantitative
-All kids brought goggles.
-Love swimming below water.
Qualitative
-Trends: 3D, video games & digital media.

Outcome

-Well received in testing.
-$2MM+ revenue in 1st year.
-Entire line of 3D pools created.
