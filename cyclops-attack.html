Cyclops Sprinkler & Rocket Launcher

Sales and Marketing Feedback

  • Small product range required for kids 6+.
  • Sales wanted non-traditional concepts.

Target Users

  • Boys, 6+.
  • Capable, sophisticated motorskills.
  • Competitive, goal-oriented.
  • Complex tastes due to video games and media.

User Research

  • Video games, X-Box and PlayStation.
  • Ben 10 most popular licensed character.
  • Punching bags, rocket launcher and water guns were favorites.

Outcome

  • Hand-held foot powered rocket launcher was highly successful in market, adopted into other products.
  • Product size & price point provided Sales with strategic positioning options for end caps and promotions.
  • Bizerk branding applied to entire product catalog for kids 6+.
