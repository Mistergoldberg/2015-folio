Ball Pop Sprinkler

Key Insights

  • Playtime research showed sprinklers are kids' favorites.
  • Parents love sprinklers and are willing to buy them.

Target Users

  • Toddler 1-3.
  • Unrefined movement, beginning to walk.
  • Driven by sensory input: new sights, feelings and sounds.
  • Fun exploring, touching, pulling, pushing, filling and dumping.

User Research

The following were consistently found in toddlers' play rooms and day cares:
  • Color & shape sorters
  • Basic puzzles

Outcome

  • Product scored top marks during focus group testing.
  • Cause & effect play pattern was a big hit with merchandisers.
